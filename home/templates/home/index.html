About Us

What are we?

The Nant Y Frith MTB is a mountain biking area in North Wales. The area is full of steep slopes and plenty of trees to catch you off guard. The good thing about Nant Y Frith is the weather very rarely ruins the riding. The tracks work well in the the dustiest of summer days, but also they work well in the wet but be warned they do get a little loose.

You might be asking what is this website for?

Well this website is for people who are looking to ride in the area. But also for people who want to be updated on new trails. The trails descriptions and locations are on the trails page. The subscription fee goes back into the woods with the help of maintenance and the building of new trails.
